#LNR #OpenCall #TheArtVitrine
**"**_Wo du bist, will ich sein"_ shows two bodies clinging to one another, forming a shared existence where each anchors the other. The surrounding wheat heightens the sense of closeness and vulnerability. The work is a tribute to a bond defined by togetherness, a connection where one cannot imagine being without the other.
